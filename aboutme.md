My name is Emmanuel Alcalá (actually: Jaime Emmanuel Alcalá Temores, which is just too much). I'm a PhD candidate in Behavior Analysis at Centro de Estudios e Investigaciones en Comportamiento (in english: Center of Studies and Research on Behavior, which is a strange name), in Guadalajara, México. 

I love all stuff related to Linux, Open Science and Free Knowledge. Academically I'm interested in behaviorism (or the umbrella name of Behavior Analysis, which is more an approach in psychology to study behavior more than a branch of science which it's own and consistent philosophy), cognitive sciences and mathematics (and how can I relate all of these). 

I studied Pharmacy, Chemistry and Biology in college (here in Mexico there is a career with all those topics, called Químico Farmacobiology) but I get bored too soon to persue more of the same, so I just turned on my interests to psychology.
