My name is Emmanuel Alcalá (actually: Jaime Emmanuel Alcalá Temores, which is just too much). I'm a PhD candidate in Behavior Analysis at Centro de Estudios e Investigaciones en Comportamiento (in english: Center of Studies and Research on Behavior, which is a strange name), in Guadalajara, México. 

I love all stuff related to Linux, Open Science and Free Knowledge. Academically I'm interested in behaviorism (or the umbrella name of Behavior Analysis, which is more an approach in psychology to study behavior than a branch of science with it's own and consistent philosophy), cognitive sciences, statistics, math and learning (a more broad topic than just psychology that includes Reinforcement Learning and Machine Learning, advanced areas or research in engineering). 

I studied Pharmacy, Chemistry and Biology in college (here in Mexico there is a career with all those topics, with the wordy name of Químico Farmacobiólogo) but I get bored too soon to persue more of the same, so I just turned on my interests into psychology.
